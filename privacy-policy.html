Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026

Data Collection

pforprofits.com does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculators run entirely in your browser (client-side JavaScript). No form submissions, no cookies for tracking, no analytics scripts that identify you.

Email Subscriptions

If you submit your email via our lead capture form, your email is stored securely via our third-party email provider for the sole purpose of sending you our property investment report. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Third-Party Services

We use GitHub Pages for hosting. GitHub's privacy policy applies to server-level data (IP addresses, request logs). We do not use Google Analytics or any behavioural tracking.

Cookies

No tracking cookies are used. Our site is cookie-free for all visitors.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@pforprofits.com.
