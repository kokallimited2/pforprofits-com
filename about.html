About pforprofits.com

pforprofits.com was built for one reason: UK property investors need better tools to analyse deals — and they shouldn't have to pay for spreadsheets that do basic maths.

We're a small independent team of property investors who got tired of clunky Excel templates and overpriced SaaS tools that do what a few lines of JavaScript can do in your browser.

What We Do

We build free, accurate, instant calculators for UK property investment analysis:

  • BRRR Calculator — Analyse Buy-Refurbish-Refinance-Rent deals
  • Rental Yield Calculator — Gross and net yield with all costs included
  • Deal Analyser — Full investment picture including ROI and breakeven

Why Free?

No signup, no data collection, no hidden upsells. All calculations run in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers. We believe property data should be freely available to everyone, whether you're buying your first buy-to-let or scaling your tenth portfolio.

Our Approach

We follow UK-specific calculations: stamp duty thresholds, typical purchase costs, realistic yield expectations. Every number is designed to give you a realistic picture of what a deal looks like in today's market.
